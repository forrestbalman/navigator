-instructions-

  1. press start
  2. pick a square, don't play until the timer finishes
  3. play, wait for arrow
  4. move in the arrows direction after the timer finishes
  5. repeat until the end

if the arrow points you off the screen, wrap around to the other side.

if the arrow is diagonal and points you off the screen, choose any square.
